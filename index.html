Talents in Spotlight - Spoken Word Artistes
There’s something powerful about spoken word—about voices that cut through the noise and find their way straight to the heart. This month, we turn our focus to the spoken word artistes who are redefining storytelling through rhythm and rhyme. From raw, personal stories to thought-provoking social commentary, they create moments that linger long after the mic is off.
Varun Grover
National Award-winning lyricist and acclaimed comedian, celebrated for his impactful work in the globally acclaimed film Masaan
An IIT engineer turned writer, Varun has clawed his way in the world of cinema and comedy on the basis of sheer talent. His lyrics, often laced with depth and authenticity, connect with the crowd on an emotional level, while his stand-up performances reflect his sharp observational humor and thought-provoking insights. A true creative force, Varun Grover continues to inspire with his unique talent and intellect.

Nayab Midha
Acclaimed Hindi poetess and the first female spoken word artiste to surpass 1 million followers on Instagram
Nayab Midha’s evocative poetry, rich with emotion and authenticity, has struck a deep chord with audiences, bringing modern relevance to traditional Hindi literature. With her powerful words and engrossing stage presence, Nayab has redefined spoken word poetry, inspiring a new generation of readers and listeners.

Johnson & Johnson’s National Sales Meet 2025 in Kolkata

VVS Laxman

Shalmali Kholgade

The J&J National Sales Meet 2025 unfolded with an inspiring mix of leadership insights and spirited entertainment, bringing together the nation’s top sales leaders for an unforgettable experience. The event struck the perfect balance between business acumen and celebration, offering attendees a refreshing mix of knowledge and enjoyment.

The evening opened with a powerful keynote by cricketing legend VVS Laxman, who engaged the audience with his stories of perseverance, strategy, and maintaining composure under pressure. His natural ability to draw parallels between his on-field experiences and real-world business challenges left a lasting impact on everyone present.

Shifting gears to a more festive note, Shalmali Kholgade delivered a show-stopping performance that had the crowd on their feet. Her lively music and magnetic stage presence filled the room with energy, creating an atmosphere of joy and camaraderie.

The thoughtful fusion of insightful discussions and entertainment ensured the event was not only impactful but also left attendees feeling motivated and ready to take on the challenges of the year ahead.

Birla Estates Project Launch, 2025

Aparshakti Khurana

Guru Randhawa

The Birla Estates Project Launch held in Delhi, brought together their channel partners for a night that celebrated new opportunities and meaningful collaboration. The event offered an engaging mix of business insights and vibrant entertainment, creating a well-rounded experience for all attendees.

Aparshakti Khurana co-hosted the evening, guiding the program with his effortless charm and keeping the flow smooth and engaging. His natural rapport with the audience ensured that every moment felt connected and purposeful.

The event beautifully combined strategy with celebration, leaving partners not only informed but also energized and ready for the journey ahead.

Hero MotoCorp Annual Family Day: A Celebration of Togetherness

Hussain Kuwajerwala

Sugandha Mishra

Vishal Malhotra

Hero MotoCorp hosted its much-anticipated Annual Family Day in Delhi, bringing together employees and their families for a day of joy, laughter, and celebration. The event served as a tribute to the hardworking individuals behind the brand while creating meaningful experiences for their loved ones.

The day was packed with engaging activities with Hussain Kuwajerwala leading as the primary host. His lively presence set the tone for a welcoming atmosphere. Comedian, singer, actress, and mimicry artist Sugandha Mishra added a lighthearted touch to the event, delivering a comedy act that had the audience laughing and enjoying every moment.

This initiative reflected Hero Moto Corp’s commitment to sustainability and encouraged kids to connect with nature while contributing to a greener future. The Annual Family Day underscored Hero Moto Corp’s commitment to valuing its people and their hard work.
